Tiffany S. Neman, Ph.D.

Survey Methodologist & Mixed-Methods Researcher

About Me

With over 10 years of professional experience in qualitative and quantitative research, I specialize in designing, conducting, and analyzing surveys to produce robust, data-driven insights. My work has resulted in multiple peer-reviewed publications and numerous conference presentations. I also serve on the executive board of the Pacific Chapter of the American Association for Public Opinion Research (PAPOR), where I contribute to advancing survey methodology. I hold a Ph.D. in Sociology from the University of Wisconsin-Madison, where I received training in survey methodology and demography, and a Bachelor's in Sociology from the University of California-Los Angeles, graduating summa cum laude.
